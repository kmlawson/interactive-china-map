Show Chinese Names

China Map Review

This interactive map allows you to practice the names of China's modern day provinces, provincial capitals, and a few other cities that are important for modern Chinese history. Keep in mind that China's provinces, autonomous regions, and directly administered cities have undergone a number of important changes so please consult other maps for earlier periods, especially before the 1960s.

Mouse over or tap on provinces and cities to show their name and check your memory. You may toggle the visibility of the Yellow River in the north and the Yangzi River in the south.

This was created with Anthropic Claude Sonnet 3.5 with Konrad Lawson at the prompt. The Wikipedia source for the basemap is here. I added cities myself. Wikipedia Commons supplied the Yellow and Yangzi river SVGs.
